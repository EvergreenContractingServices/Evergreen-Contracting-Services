About Us

Evergreen Contracting Services has been improving homes across Fairfax County since 2005. Founded by a tradesman who learned the craft from the ground up — beginning as a carpenter’s apprentice before building a career across residential and commercial construction — Evergreen brings a hands-on standard to every project.

We are not a national franchise or a call-center contractor. We are a local, family-run team that shows up on time, communicates clearly, and takes pride in the finished result. Whether you're updating a single bathroom or renovating your entire home, you'll work directly with experienced professionals from first call to final walkthrough.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

What is your primary service area?

We proudly serve Fairfax County, Arlington County, Prince William County, Loudoun County, and surrounding Northern Virginia areas including Burke, Vienna, Alexandria, and Falls Church.

Are you fully licensed and certified?

Yes, Evergreen Contracting Services LLC holds a Class "B" Home Improvements license with VA DPOR (License #2705105936) and is an EPA Lead Certified Renovator with an A+ BBB Accreditation rating.

How do your quotes and consulting process work?

We evaluate your complete home layout and understand your renovation goals before starting any physical work to ensure precise alignment. We offer free, clear, no-pressure in-home estimates.
